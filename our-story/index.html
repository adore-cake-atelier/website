Our Story

We're two friends, Anastasia and Daria — and just two years ago, we were home bakers, until one day we decided it was time to create a space of our own. A place where we could make cakes and connect with our customers in person.

Finding the perfect location wasn't easy, but it was absolutely worth it. We found our beautiful space — second home — which we call Adore Cake Atelier.

Two months after opening, another Anastasia joined our team as our content and brand manager.

All of our cakes are handmade on-site using natural ingredients and tailored to your individual requests. We create cakes for every occasion: birthdays, kids' parties, weddings, gender reveals, Christmas/New Year, Easter, Valentine's Day, International Women's Day — basically any event you can think of!

You can also always stop by our atelier and enjoy a slice of cake from our display.

We've only been open for a year and a half, but we've already built a strong and loving community of regular customers who truly support us and what we do.
